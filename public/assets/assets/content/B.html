B

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Flat Palm: All four fingers are extended straight up and held tightly together.
  • check_circle Thumb Position: The thumb is folded across the palm, resting near the base of the fingers.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'B' with these similar signs:

  • 4 Number 4

    The thumb is tucked the same way, but the four fingers are spread apart.

  • 5 Letter F

    The index and thumb touch to form a circle, only three fingers are up.

  • U Letter U

    Only the index and middle fingers are up, ring and pinky are folded.
