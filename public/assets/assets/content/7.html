7

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Thumb to Ring Finger: The tip of the thumb touches the tip of the ring finger.
  • check_circle Extended Digits: The index, middle, and pinky fingers remain extended.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: Unlike counting 1-5, the palm faces the viewer (palm-out).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '7' with these similar signs:

  • 6 Number 6

    The thumb touches the pinky finger. For '7', the thumb touches the ring finger.

  • 8 Number 8

    The thumb touches the middle finger. '7' and '8' are frequently swapped by beginners.

  • 9 Number 9

    The thumb touches the index finger. For '7', the thumb is two fingers away from the index finger.
