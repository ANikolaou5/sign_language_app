5

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Full Extension: All five digits (thumb and four fingers) are extended and spread wide apart.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: For counting 1-5 in ASL, the palm faces the signer (palm-in).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '5' with these similar signs:

  • B Letter B

    Fingers are pressed together and the thumb is tucked. For '5', fingers are spread and the thumb is out.

  • C Letter C

    The hand is curved into a semi-circle. '5' must be flat and spread.
