E

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle The Claw: All four fingers are curled tightly, with the tips resting on or near the top of the thumb.
  • check_circle Thumb Position: The thumb is folded horizontally across the palm, tucked under the curled fingers.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'E' with these similar signs:

  • A Letter A

    The thumb is straight up and pressed against the side of the index finger.

  • S Letter S

    Thumb is crossed over the front of the fingers.

  • T Letter T

    Thumb is tucked under the index finger.
