6

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Thumb to Pinky: The tip of the thumb touches the tip of the pinky finger.
  • check_circle Three Up: The index, middle, and ring fingers remain extended and spread apart.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: Unlike counting 1-5, the palm faces the viewer (palm-out).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '6' with these similar signs:

  • 9 Number 9

    The thumb also touches a single finger, but it is the index (making the 'OK' sign).

  • F Letter F

    The thumb touches the index finger. For '6', the thumb must touch the pinky finger.

  • W Letter W

    Shares the exact same handshape as '6'. It is distinguished purely by context (counting vs. spelling).
