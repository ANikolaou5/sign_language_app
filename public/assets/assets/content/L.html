L

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Right Angle: The index finger points straight up while the thumb points straight out to the side, forming a 90° angle.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: The palm should face forward, away from the signer, making the "L" shape clear to the viewer.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'L' with these similar signs:

  • 1 Number 1

    The index is up, but the thumb is tucked in against the other fingers rather than sticking out.

  • D Letter D

    The index points up, but the thumb touches the fingers to form a circle at the base.

  • G Letter G

    Uses a similar thumb/index shape, but the hand is rotated sideways across the body.
