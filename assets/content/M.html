M

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Three-Finger Hump: The thumb is tucked under the index, middle, and ring fingers.
  • check_circle Thumb Peeking: The tip of the thumb should peek out between the ring finger and the pinky.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'M' with these similar signs:

  • N Letter N

    The thumb is tucked under only two fingers (index and middle). 'M' uses three.

  • S Letter S

    A standard fist where the thumb is over the fingers, not tucked inside them.

  • T Letter T

    The thumb is tucked under only one finger (the index finger).
