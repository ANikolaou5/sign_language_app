Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Index Extension: The index finger is pointing straight up while all other fingers are folded into the palm.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: For counting 1-5 in ASL, the palm faces the signer (palm-in).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '1' with these similar signs:

  • D Letter D

    The palm faces out and the thumb touches the tips of the folded fingers to form a circle.

  • X Letter X

    The index finger is hooked or bent at the knuckle. For '1', the finger must be straight.

  • Z Letter Z

    Uses the same handshape but moves to trace a "Z" in the air. '1' is held perfectly still.
