F

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle The "OK" Sign: The tips of the index finger and thumb touch to form a circle.
  • check_circle Three Fingers Up: The middle, ring, and pinky fingers point straight up and are held tightly together.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'F' with these similar signs:

  • 9 Number 9

    Uses the same pinch, but the three fingers are spread apart.

  • D Letter D

    The index finger is up, the thumb touches the other three fingers.

  • W Letter W

    Three fingers are up, but the thumb and pinky touch (instead of thumb and index).
