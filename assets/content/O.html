O

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle The Perfect Circle: All fingers curve down to meet the thumb, forming a clear "O" shape.
  • check_circle Tip Contact: The fingertips should be touching or very close to the tip of the thumb, creating a hollow center.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'O' with these similar signs:

  • C Letter C

    The hand forms a semi-circle, but the fingers and thumb do not touch.

  • D Letter D

    While the bottom fingers form a circle, the index finger must point straight up.

  • E Letter E

    The fingers are scrunched and flattened. 'O' must be open and rounded like a ball.
