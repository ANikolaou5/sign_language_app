Q

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Downward Pinch: The index finger and thumb are extended in a "pinch" shape, pointing straight down.
  • check_circle The Gap: Similar to 'G', there is a small space between the parallel thumb and index finger.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'Q' with these similar signs:

  • 8 Number 8

    Thumb touches the middle finger while other fingers are spread out. for 'Q', the thumb touches the index while others are folded.

  • F Letter F

    The thumb and index touch to form a circle, but the other three fingers are pointing up. 'Q' has them folded.

  • P Letter P

    Both point down, but 'P' includes the middle finger. 'Q' only uses the thumb and index.
