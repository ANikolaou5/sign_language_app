W

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Three-Finger Extension: The index, middle, and ring fingers are extended and spread apart.
  • check_circle Pinky Takedown: The thumb holds down the pinky finger only, resting against the pinky's nail or tip.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'W' with these similar signs:

  • 6 Number 6

    Shares the exact same handshape as 'W'. It is distinguished purely by context (counting vs. spelling).

  • F Letter F

    Also has three fingers up, but the thumb and index finger touch. 'W' has the thumb and pinky touching.

  • V Letter V

    Only the index and middle fingers are up. 'W' adds the ring finger to the shape.
