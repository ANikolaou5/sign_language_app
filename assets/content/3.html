3

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Thumb Inclusion: The thumb, index, and middle fingers are extended.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: For counting 1-5 in ASL, the palm faces the signer (palm-in).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '3' with these similar signs:

  • 6 Number 6

    Uses three fingers (index, middle, ring) but the thumb touches the pinky.

  • K Letter K

    Also uses thumb, index, and middle, but the thumb touches the middle finger and the palm faces out.

  • W Letter W

    Uses index, middle, and ring fingers with the thumb tucked. Number 3 must use the thumb.
