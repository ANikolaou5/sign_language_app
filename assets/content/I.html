I

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Pinky Up: The pinky finger points straight up toward the sky.
  • check_circle Closed Fist: The other three fingers are folded into the palm, with the thumb crossed over them.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'I' with these similar signs:

  • D Letter D

    The index finger is up. For 'I', it is the pinky finger.

  • J Letter J

    Starts with the 'I' handshape, but adds a "swooping" motion like drawing a hook in the air.

  • Y Letter Y

    Both the pinky and the thumb are extended. For 'I', the thumb stays tucked.
