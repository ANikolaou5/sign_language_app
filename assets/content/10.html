10

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Thumb Up: Make a fist with the thumb pointing straight up (similar to a "thumbs up" gesture).
  • check_circle The Wiggle: The hand rotates slightly or the thumb wiggles side-to-side to indicate the number.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: Unlike counting 1-5, the palm faces the viewer (palm-out).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '10' with these similar signs:

  • A Letter A

    Uses a similar handshape, but is held still and the thumb is usually tucked against the side of the fist.

  • S Letter S

    A closed fist where the thumb is crossed over the front. For '10', the thumb must be standing up.
