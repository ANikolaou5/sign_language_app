P

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Downward Point: The index and middle fingers are extended and point down toward the ground.
  • check_circle Thumb Anchor: Similar to 'K', the thumb tip touches the middle of the middle finger.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'P' with these similar signs:

  • H Letter H

    The fingers are together and point sideways. for 'P', they are spread and point down.

  • K Letter K

    Uses the exact same handshape, but the fingers point upward toward the sky.

  • Q Letter Q

    Both point down, but 'Q' uses the thumb and index only (like a pinch). 'P' uses two fingers plus the thumb.
