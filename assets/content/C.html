C

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Curved Shape: The hand forms a semi-circle, resembling the shape of the printed letter 'C'.
  • check_circle Finger Position: All fingers are held together and curved toward the palm, but they do not touch the thumb.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'C' with these similar signs:

  • O Letter O

    The fingers and thumb touch each other to form a complete, closed circle.

  • E Letter Q

    The hand uses a similar curve, but it is tilted downward toward the ground.

  • E Letter X

    Only the index finger is curved, the other three fingers are folded into a fist.
