R

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Crossed Fingers: The middle finger crosses over the back of the index finger (like the common "fingers crossed" gesture for luck).
  • check_circle Tucked Thumb: The thumb stays tucked into the palm, resting over the ring and pinky fingers.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'R' with these similar signs:

  • 2 Number 2

    The index and middle fingers are extended and separated. for 'R', those same fingers must be tightly crossed.

  • U Letter U

    The index and middle fingers are straight and pressed together, not crossed.

  • V Letter V

    The index and middle fingers are straight but spread apart in a "V" shape.
