J

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Dynamic Motion: Unlike most letters, 'J' involves movement. You draw the shape of the letter in the air.
  • check_circle The Hook: Start with the 'I' handshape (pinky up) and scoop your hand downward and upward in a "U" or "J" motion.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'J' with these similar signs:

  • I Letter I

    The handshape is the same, but 'I' is completely still. 'J' must have the "swoop."

  • Y Letter Y

    If you accidentally extend your thumb while drawing the 'J', it can look like a moving 'Y'. Keep the thumb tucked!

  • Z Letter Z

    Both move, but 'Z' is drawn with the index finger. 'J' is drawn with the pinky finger.
