D

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Pointer Up: The index finger points straight up toward the sky.
  • check_circle The Circle: The thumb and the other three fingers (middle, ring, pinky) touch tips to form a circle.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'D' with these similar signs:

  • 1 Number 1

    The index is up, but the other fingers are tucked into a tight fist rather than forming a circle.

  • F Letter F

    The index and thumb touch to form the circle, while the other three fingers stay up.

  • I Letter U

    Both the index and middle fingers point up together, the thumb stays tucked over the ring finger.
