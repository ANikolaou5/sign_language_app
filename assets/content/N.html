N

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Two-Finger Hump: The thumb is tucked under the index and middle fingers only.
  • check_circle Thumb Position: The tip of the thumb peeks out between the middle finger and the ring finger.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'N' with these similar signs:

  • M Letter M

    The thumb is tucked under three fingers (index, middle, and ring). 'N' only uses two.

  • S Letter S

    A standard fist where the thumb is over the fingers, not tucked inside them.

  • T Letter T

    The thumb is tucked under only the index finger. 'N' uses both index and middle.
