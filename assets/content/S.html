S

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle The Natural Fist: All four fingers are folded tightly into the palm.
  • check_circle Thumb Over: The thumb is crossed directly over the front of the fingers, usually resting across the middle knuckles.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'S' with these similar signs:

  • A Letter A

    The thumb is straight up and pressed against the side of the index finger.

  • E Letter E

    Fingers curled, thumb folded horizontally across the palm.

  • T Letter T

    Thumb is tucked under the index finger.
