A

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Fist Shape: Four fingers folded tightly into the palm.
  • check_circle Thumb Position: Thumb stays straight up against the index finger.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'A' with these similar signs:

  • S Letter S

    Thumb is crossed over the front of the fingers.

  • T Letter T

    Thumb is tucked under the index finger.

  • E Letter E

    Fingers curled, thumb folded horizontally across the palm.
