H

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Two Fingers Sideways: The index and middle fingers are extended and point horizontally across the body.
  • check_circle Closed Fingers: Unlike some other signs, the two extended fingers must be touching each other.

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse 'H' with these similar signs:

  • G Letter G

    Uses the same sideways angle, but only the index finger is extended.

  • P Letter P Letter K

    Two fingers point up, but they are spread apart in a "V" shape with the thumb touching the middle finger.

  • U Letter U

    The index and middle fingers are together, but they point straight up rather than sideways.
