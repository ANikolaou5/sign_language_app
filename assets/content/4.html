4

American Sign Language

Key Characteristics

  • check_circle Four-Finger Extension: Index, middle, ring, and pinky fingers are extended and slightly spread.
  • check_circle Thumb Tuck: The thumb is folded across the palm, tucked away from the other fingers.
  • check_circle Palm Orientation: For counting 1-5 in ASL, the palm faces the signer (palm-in).

Common Clarifications

Don't confuse '4' with this similar sign:

  • B Letter B

    The four fingers are pressed together and the palm faces out. For '4', fingers are spread and palm faces in.
